What is the Daily Savings App?
The Daily Savings App helps you track your daily savings and withdrawals, manage your savings history, and visualize savings with a calendar.
How do I save and withdraw money in the app?
You can save or withdraw money by selecting the transaction type, entering an amount, and clicking the "Submit" button.
Can I delete or clear all transactions?
Yes, you can delete individual transactions or clear all transactions using the "Delete" button in each row or the "Clear All" button.
